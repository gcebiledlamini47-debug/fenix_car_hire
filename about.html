About Fenix Car Hire

Fenix Car Hire is a professional vehicle rental company based in the Kingdom of Eswatini.
The company provides reliable, safe and cost-effective mobility solutions to corporate,
government, NGO and private clients across the country.The company operate from Lilanga
Complex, Unit B, Sidvwashini, is committed to delivering dependable transport services
supported by a well maintained fleet and customer-focused operations.
